Privacy Policy

FocusKit collects no personal data. Everything the app stores stays entirely on your device.

FocusKit by NyxLast updated: June 2026

The short version: FocusKit doesn't collect, transmit, or store any information about you on any server. It doesn't have a server. Your data is yours and it stays on your phone.

Data we collect

None. FocusKit does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or any other information from you or your device.

Data stored on your device

FocusKit stores the following data locally on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms:

  • Your task notes (what you're working on during focus sessions)
  • Session history (dates and durations of your focus sessions)
  • App settings (high-contrast mode, reduce-motion preferences, session length)
  • Progress data ("you showed up N days" counter)

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any cloud service controlled by Nyx. It is not accessible to us.

Third-party services

FocusKit does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no third-party services embedded in the app that receive your data.

The app does not make any network requests at runtime. You can verify this by reviewing the source code: github.com/Costder/FocusKit.

Permissions

FocusKit may request the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications — to deliver a gentle sound when your focus session ends. You can disable this in Android settings.
  • Vibration — to provide haptic feedback at session end if desired.

No other permissions are requested.

Children's privacy

FocusKit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app collects no information from anyone, of any age.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all FocusKit data at any time by uninstalling the app. Uninstalling removes all stored data permanently.

Changes to this policy

If FocusKit ever adds features that collect or transmit data (such as an opt-in cloud backup feature), this privacy policy will be updated before those features are released and users will be clearly informed. Any data-collecting features will be opt-in only.
