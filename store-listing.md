## Full description (<=4000 chars)

Most ADHD focus apps were built for brains that already focus on command. FocusKit was built from the other direction — reading what the ADHD community actually asks for, then building exactly that. Nothing else.

★ BODY DOUBLING COMPANION
Working next to another person makes the impossible suddenly possible. That's body doubling, and it's one of the most-cited ADHD strategies that actually works. FocusKit's companion sits with you for the entire session — a calm, quiet presence that makes starting (and staying started) feel less like going it alone.

★ TIME-BLINDNESS TIMER
A number ticking down in the corner doesn't help if you don't feel time passing. FocusKit's timer is a visual ring that visibly drains during your session. You glance up and your brain registers how much time is left — not just reads it. Choose 5, 15, 25, or 45 minutes.

★ ONE-TAP TASK CAPTURE
The thought shows up. Tap once and it's saved. No projects, no priorities, no planning session before you can start. Capture the task in under two seconds before it disappears.

★ NO-SHAME PROGRESS
FocusKit doesn't count streaks. It counts the days you showed up — a number that only goes up, never resets, never punishes you for a missed day. Showing up matters. That's all it tracks.

★ SENSORY-CALM DESIGN
High-contrast mode, reduce-motion option, quiet colors throughout. No notification badges competing for attention. No pop-ups asking you to rate the app mid-session. Designed not to add to the noise.

★ PRIVATE BY DEFAULT — AND WE MEAN IT
No account. No sign-up. Nothing leaves your phone. Your tasks and focus history live only on your device, not on a server somewhere. No ads, no behavioral tracking, no selling your data. The full codebase is public on GitHub — read every line if you want to verify this.

---

FocusKit is for ADHD adults who've tried other focus apps and quit them. Not because the strategy was wrong. Because the app wasn't built for how their brain actually works.

Free to start. Fair pricing if you want more. No dark patterns.
