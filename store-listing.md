## Full description

Most focus apps are built for neurotypical brains — endless fields, guilt-tripping
streaks, forced accounts, and ads everywhere. FocusKit is built for the way ADHD
brains actually work, and it keeps everything private on your device.

★ BODY DOUBLING
Start (and stay started) with a calm on-screen companion. Working alongside a gentle
presence makes it easier to begin the thing you've been avoiding.

★ SEE TIME PASSING (time-blindness support)
A big, visual timer that depletes as your session runs — so you can *feel* time, not
just read numbers. Pick 5, 15, 25, or 45 minutes.

★ NO-FRICTION TASKS
Capture the one thing on your mind in a single tap. No projects, no priorities, no
overwhelm. Tap one task to "focus" and it follows you into your session.

★ NO-SHAME PROGRESS
We don't punish broken streaks. FocusKit celebrates *showing up* — your sessions,
your minutes, and the days you turned up, whenever they happen.

★ CALM BY DESIGN
A low-stimulation, sensory-friendly interface with high-contrast and reduce-motion
options.

★ PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
No account. No ads. No tracking. No cloud. Everything stays on your device — forever.

Made by Nyx — software that respects your attention.
