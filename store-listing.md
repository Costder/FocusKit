## Full description (<=4000 chars)

Most ADHD focus apps were built for brains that already focus on command. FocusKit was built from the other direction — reading what the ADHD community actually asks for, then building exactly that. Nothing else.

★ BODY DOUBLING COMPANION
Working next to another person makes the impossible suddenly possible. That is body doubling, and it is one of the most-cited ADHD strategies that actually works. FocusKit companion sits with you for the entire session — a calm, quiet presence that makes starting (and staying started) feel less like going it alone.

★ TIME-BLINDNESS TIMER
A number ticking down in the corner does not help if you do not feel time passing. FocusKit timer is a visual ring that visibly drains during your session. You glance up and your brain registers how much time is left — not just reads it. Choose 5, 15, 25, or 45 minutes.

★ ONE-TAP TASK CAPTURE
The thought shows up. Tap once and it is saved. No projects, no priorities, no planning session before you can start. Capture the task in under two seconds before it disappears.

★ NO-SHAME PROGRESS
FocusKit does not count streaks. It counts the days you showed up — a number that only goes up, never resets, never punishes you for a missed day. Showing up matters. That is all it tracks.

★ SENSORY-CALM DESIGN
High-contrast mode, reduce-motion option, quiet colors throughout. No notification badges competing for attention. No pop-ups asking you to rate the app mid-session. Designed not to add to the noise.

★ PRIVATE BY DEFAULT — AND WE MEAN IT
No account. No sign-up. Nothing leaves your phone. Your tasks and focus history live only on your device, not on a server somewhere. No ads, no behavioral tracking, no selling your data. The full codebase is public on GitHub — read every line if you want to verify this.

## DATA SAFETY SECTION (Play Console → App content → Data safety)

Google requires you to disclose all data collection. FocusKit collects nothing.
Fill in the Data Safety form as follows:

**Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?**
→ NO (select "No, I don't collect or share any of the required data types")

**Is all of the user data collected by your app encrypted in transit?**
→ N/A (no data is transmitted)

**Do you provide a way for users to request that their data is deleted?**
→ Select "Yes" — users can delete their data by uninstalling the app. 
  OR select "No" and explain: all data is local-only and deletes on uninstall.

The resulting "Data safety" box on the Play Store listing should show:
✓ No data shared with third parties
✓ No data collected
✓ Data is encrypted in transit (or N/A)
